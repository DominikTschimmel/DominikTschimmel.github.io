Throughout my preparations for the exams of the master's degree in physics, I have been summarizing some subjects in LaTex documents. After some time, the summaries became more or less extensive notes, containing some of the major topics for the respective subject. However, these notes should not be mistaken with introductory or complete textbooks/ lecture notes about the subject.

During the literature research for my bachelor' s thesis, I encountered numerous pay walls and membership restrictions. As much as I do understand the need for proper payment, I myself believe that science has to be available for everyone. For that reason I have decided to upload the notes that have seen enough care to have proper (enough) citation.
